Tabletop roleplaying games, like D&D, are collaborative activities. Players come from a variety of different roleplaying backgrounds, with different levels of familiarity, proficiency and expectations. Put simply: different people enjoy different aspects of the hobby. And it’s everyone’s job - whether player or game master - to collaborate and help make sure that everyone has a good time.

In this spirit of collaboration, and to help ensure that everyone is on the same page in terms of what to expect and in knowing who your fellow players are, I find it important for everyone to create characters together as a group.

I would like to arrange a time for everyone to meet up, introduce ourselves and make characters. As with any activity which involves coordinating the schedules of many people, finding a time which everyone can attend is always a tricky proposition.

To help facilitate this, I have put together a Doodle listing possible times to meet up. Please fill it out and after everyone has entered their info, I’ll pick the best time available for the character creation session. Once we have a set time, I’ll look into available venues in the area (game shops or whatnot).

I look forward to seeing everyone at the game!
