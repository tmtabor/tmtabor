Trystell

Trystell is a fantasy world, which is just emerging from its version of the medieval era. This campaign focuses on the island of Cebu and the surrounding regions. Cebu is located in the Rasonic Ocean, in the northern hemisphere of the world. The island is huge, somewhere between the size of Madagascar and Australia. At the northern points, it touches the Circle of Na, Trystell's equivalent to the Arctic Circle. At its lower points it's solidly temperate.

To the southwest, across the ocean, is the empire of Grichom, a thriving country of humans with a dragonborn minority. This country founded Lalog colony on Cebu as part of a crusade, almost a generation ago.

To the southwest, along the coast, are a scattering of elven city-states, remnants of empires that collapsed a century ago or more. These city-states are mercantile forces in their own right, but their control doesn't extend far from the city walls. Further inland there is a land of petty warlords and turmoil.
