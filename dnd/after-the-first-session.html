As a reminder, everyone leveled ...

The first session is over and I will more-or-less consider it a success. I also want to have some sort of web presence for the campaign, this way there's a place for you guys to go to check up on campaign planning, loot, etc.

As a reminder, everyone leveled up to Level 2 at the end of last session. This is good because not only does it give people more character options, but it also gives all of the characters more hit points, which means they're sturdier and less likely to be dropped by a single hit or two in combat. Please have your spells or new features picked by the start of next session. If you want to browse available spells, the Fifth Edition SRD is available here.

Speaking of next session, just as a reminder, it will be Saturday, October 8th at 5 pm, at the same place as before.

Finally, here is a list of the loot acquired over the course of last session (some of which may already have been traded for healing potions):

  • Leather Armor x10
  • Mace x1
  • Scimitar x9
  • Light Crossbow x9
  • Crossbow Bolts x56
  • 17 cp, 14 sp, 10 ep, 10 gp, 3 pp
  • 7 citrine gems
